We are church that believes in Jesus Christ

The Pathway Way to the wisdom... "That the God of our Lord Jesus Christ, the Father of glory, may give unto you the spirit of wisdom and revelation in the knowledge of him:"(Eph.1:17.)

There are three things we delights in, and which are delightful to find and to all people;

  • Concord between Brothers,
  • Friendship between Neighbours and
  • Wife and Husband who live Happily together.

There are three sorts of people we hates and whose existence we consider an outrage;

  • The Poor Swollen with Pride,
  • The Rich who is a liar, and
  • Adulterous Old man who has no sense.
